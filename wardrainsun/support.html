Product Care & Use

After use in rain, open the umbrella fully and let it air-dry before storing — never store it wet or folded while damp, as this causes fabric and frame deterioration. Keep it away from prolonged direct sunlight when not in use. Avoid forcing the mechanism if it feels stiff; a light wipe with a dry cloth on the runner usually helps.

Minor fading over years of UV exposure is considered normal wear and is not covered by warranty. Significant early fading (within 6 months of normal use) may qualify — email us with a photo and we'll assess it.

Make sure the shaft is clean and dry — debris or moisture near the runner is the most common cause. If the button mechanism itself is faulty and the umbrella is registered under warranty, contact us for a replacement.
